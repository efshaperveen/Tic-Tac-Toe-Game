About Tic-Tac-Toe

1. Game Overview

    A two-player game.
    Players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid.
    One player uses X and the other uses O.

2. Objective

    The goal is to form a straight line of three of your symbols (X or O).
    The line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

3. Rules

    Players alternate turns.
    A player can only mark an empty cell.
    The game ends when: A player aligns three of their symbols. The grid is full, resulting in a draw if no one wins.

4. Game Flow

    Step 1: Decide who will use X and who will use O.
    Step 2: Players take turns marking cells.
    Step 3: Check for a winner or a draw after each move.
  • Step 4: Declare the resut (Win/Draw).
